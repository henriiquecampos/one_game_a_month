# Bard's Lesson

A game about a bard and his mission to sharpen his skills. Currently he needs to identify the pitch of the notes he hears from the environment.

![Fux, the Fox](https://i.imgur.com/nDeWooK.png)

The player must match the notes of sang by birds in the forest, they will guide the main character in his mission.
